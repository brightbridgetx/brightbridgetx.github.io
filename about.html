About Bright Bridge

Bright Bridge is a full service concierge company for seniors in the Austin metro area. We provide different non-medical services to help our seniors live a more independent lifestyle. We work with our client, their families, or guardians to make sure their needs are being met.

Nancy Wittenburg, the owner of Bright Bridge, is a native Austinite and local Dripping Springs resident. She is excited to have the opportunity to help people as they transition through different stages of their lives. Her strength lies not only in her reliability, but also in her creative, informed problem-solving skills.

After several years working in the Senior Living market as a Marketing Liaison and community Activities Director, she decided to do what she loves most, being there to assist her senior friends. With her compassionate and generous heart, Nancy began a new chapter in her life-Bright Bridge. Bright Bridge was born to give families peace of mind knowing that their loved ones are being cared for, and to enlighten the day of our seniors.

“Nancy is honest, reliable and a very safe driver. She is always ready to help! All in all, she is my angel and friend, and she can be yours too! When you need any kind of help, call Nancy.” - Jean F.

In her spare time, Nancy loves spending time with her husband Mike and family. She is proud of her two grown daughters and high school son. On weekends you can find Nancy at their family ranch, relaxing in the peaceful, quiet beauty of the Texas hill country, and enjoying her newfound passion of observing her momma cows and calves.

Feel free to call Nancy anytime; she would be glad to hear from you.
